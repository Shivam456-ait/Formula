Class 10 Chemistry Complete Formula Sheet

🔥 Mole Concept

Moles (n) = Given Mass / Molar Mass
Mass = Moles × Molar Mass
Moles = Number of Particles / 6.022 × 10²³
Number of Particles = Moles × 6.022 × 10²³
Molar Mass = Sum of Atomic Masses

⚗️ Chemical Reactions

Combination: A + B → AB
Decomposition: AB → A + B
Displacement: A + BC → AC + B
Double Displacement: AB + CD → AD + CB
Oxidation = Gain of Oxygen
Reduction = Loss of Oxygen

⚡ Acids, Bases & Salts

pH = -log[H⁺]
Neutral Solution → pH = 7
Acid + Base → Salt + Water
Strong Acid → High H⁺ concentration
Strong Base → High OH⁻ concentration

⚙️ Metals & Non-Metals

Ionic Bond → Transfer of Electrons
Covalent Bond → Sharing of Electrons
Reactivity Series:
K > Na > Ca > Mg > Al > Zn > Fe > Pb > H > Cu > Hg > Ag > Au

🔥 Carbon & Its Compounds

Covalency of Carbon = 4
Alkane = CnH2n+2
Alkene = CnH2n
Alkyne = CnH2n-2
Ethanol = C2H5OH
Ethanoic Acid = CH3COOH

💧 Concentration & Solutions

Molarity (M) = Moles / Volume (L)
Normality (N) = Gram Equivalent / Volume (L)
Mass % = (Mass of Solute / Mass of Solution) × 100
Volume % = (Volume of Solute / Volume of Solution) × 100
C₁V₁ = C₂V₂
