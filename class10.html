🧪 Mole Concept

Moles (n) = Given Mass / Molar Mass

Mass = Moles × Molar Mass

Moles = Number of Particles / 6.022 × 10²³

Number of Particles = Moles × 6.022 × 10²³

Molar Mass = Sum of Atomic Masses

⚗ Concentration & Solutions

Molarity (M) = Moles of Solute / Volume (L)

Molality (m) = Moles of Solute / Mass of Solvent (kg)

Normality (N) = Gram Equivalent / Volume (L)

Strength = Molarity × Molar Mass

Mass % = (Mass of Solute / Mass of Solution) × 100

Volume % = (Volume of Solute / Volume of Solution) × 100

C₁V₁ = C₂V₂
