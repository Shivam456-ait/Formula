Class 9 Chemistry Important Formulas

Measurement & Density

Density = Mass / Volume

Mass = Density × Volume

Volume = Mass / Density

Motion

Speed = Distance / Time

Distance = Speed × Time

Time = Distance / Speed

Acceleration = (Final Velocity − Initial Velocity) / Time

Work & Energy

Work = Force × Distance

Force = Mass × Acceleration

Kinetic Energy = ½ mv²

Potential Energy = mgh

Power = Work / Time

Atomic Structure

Number of Protons = Atomic Number

Number of Electrons = Atomic Number

Number of Neutrons = Mass Number − Atomic Number

Valency = Electrons Lost or Gained

Mole Concept

Moles = Given Mass / Molar Mass

Number of Particles = Moles × 6.022 × 10²³

Molar Mass = Sum of Atomic Masses

Solutions & Percentage

Percentage = (Part / Whole) × 100

Mass % = (Mass of Solute / Mass of Solution) × 100

Volume % = (Volume of Solute / Volume of Solution) × 100

C₁V₁ = C₂V₂
