Class 9 Chemistry Complete Formula Sheet

🔬 Matter & Its Nature

Density = Mass / Volume

Mass = Density × Volume

Volume = Mass / Density

Change in Density = ΔMass / ΔVolume

⚡ Motion & Basic Physics Used in Chemistry

Speed = Distance / Time

Distance = Speed × Time

Time = Distance / Speed

Acceleration = (Final Velocity − Initial Velocity) / Time

Force = Mass × Acceleration

Work = Force × Distance

Power = Work / Time

Kinetic Energy = ½ mv²

Potential Energy = mgh

⚛️ Atomic Structure

Atomic Number (Z) = Number of Protons

Number of Electrons = Atomic Number

Mass Number (A) = Protons + Neutrons

Neutrons = Mass Number − Atomic Number

Valency = Electrons Lost or Gained

Isotopes → Same Z, Different Mass Number

Isobars → Same Mass Number, Different Z

🧪 Mole Concept Basics

Moles = Given Mass / Molar Mass

Number of Particles = Moles × 6.022 × 10²³

Molar Mass = Sum of Atomic Masses

Mass = Moles × Molar Mass

Moles = Number of Particles / 6.022 × 10²³

🔥 Chemical Reactions

Law of Conservation of Mass → Mass Reactants = Mass Products

Percentage Yield = (Actual Yield / Theoretical Yield) × 100

Rate of Reaction = Change in Concentration / Time

🌡️ Heat & Temperature

Heat (Q) = m × c × ΔT

ΔT = Final Temperature − Initial Temperature

Specific Heat Capacity (c) = Q / (m × ΔT)

🧴 Solutions & Concentration

Percentage = (Part / Whole) × 100

Mass % = (Mass of Solute / Mass of Solution) × 100

Volume % = (Volume of Solute / Volume of Solution) × 100

C₁V₁ = C₂V₂
